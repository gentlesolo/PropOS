PropOS Agent — Your Real Estate Field Command Centre

PropOS Agent puts your entire CRM, call management, and AI coaching in your pocket. Built exclusively for real estate agents and marketers who spend their day in the field.

CALLS WITH AI INTELLIGENCE
• Make and receive VoIP calls from your dedicated business number — never use your personal number again
• Every call is automatically recorded, transcribed, and summarised by AI
• Post-call action items are converted into tasks with one tap
• Live AI coaching during calls: objection detection, suggested responses, urgency signals

UNIFIED MESSAGING
• Reply to WhatsApp, SMS, and email leads from one inbox
• AI reply suggestions: tap to generate a professional response in seconds
• Communication Coach: scores your message before you send it and suggests rewrites

FULL CRM ACCESS
• Your complete contact list, deal stages, and activity timeline — always synced
• Sentiment trend chart: see how each lead's interest has evolved across all calls
• Add voice or text notes instantly from any contact screen

VIEWINGS MADE EASY
• Today's viewing schedule with property and client details
• One-tap check-in records your arrival time
• Log viewing outcomes and notes — synced instantly to the web platform

TASK MANAGEMENT
• Today's tasks and overdue items front and centre every morning
• Swipe to complete or snooze — tasks from call summaries appear automatically

TEAM INTELLIGENCE (Managers)
• Team call leaderboard and activity dashboard
• Flag calls for coaching review — with notes
• Benchmark your performance against team percentiles

PropOS Agent works seamlessly alongside the PropOS web platform. All data stays in sync in real time.
